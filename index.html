Debated or ignored. Protested or symbols of protest. Demolished or revered. Recognized as monuments or not.

These are 16 controversial, localized monuments, each one a case study in the global practice of memorializing and constructing static history in an ever-evolving present.

The Debates While every monument has a unique history and locality, their role as monument unites them in a global practice. We have combined these monuments into chapters to situate connect them to cross-border debates around memorialization, decolonization, and public art.
